Our Amenities

Everything you need for comfortable living

Living Amenities

Furnished Rooms

All rooms come fully furnished with comfortable beds, wardrobes, study desks, and chairs. No need to bring your own furniture.

Comfortable Bedding

Premium quality mattresses, pillows, and bed linens provided. Regular cleaning and replacement ensures hygiene.

Housekeeping

Daily cleaning service keeps your room and common areas spotless. Professional staff maintains high cleanliness standards.

AC Rooms

Air-conditioned rooms available for comfortable living in all weather conditions. Temperature control for your comfort.

LED Lighting

Well-lit rooms with energy-efficient LED lights. Proper lighting for study and relaxation.

Ventilation

Well-ventilated rooms with windows for fresh air circulation. Healthy living environment maintained.

Food & Utilities

Nutritious Meals

Fresh, hygienic, and nutritious meals served three times a day. Varied menu with vegetarian options. Special dietary requirements accommodated.

RO Water

Pure RO water available 24/7 for drinking. Water purifiers installed on each floor for easy access.

Power Backup

Uninterrupted power supply with backup generators. No power cuts to disrupt your daily routine.

High-Speed Wi-Fi

Fast and reliable internet connectivity throughout the facility. Perfect for online classes, work, and entertainment.

Hot Water

24/7 hot water supply for bathing. Geysers installed in all bathrooms for your comfort.

Common Kitchen

Shared kitchen facility available for residents who prefer to cook. Equipped with basic cooking appliances.

Safety & Security

CCTV Monitoring

Complete CCTV surveillance covering all common areas, entrances, and corridors. 24/7 monitoring for your safety.

Biometric Entry

Secure biometric access control system. Only authorized residents can enter the facility. Visitor management system in place.

Warden Support

24/7 warden support available for any assistance. Trained staff to handle emergencies and resident concerns.

Security Guards

Round-the-clock security personnel on duty. Regular patrols and strict access control maintained.

Emergency Response

Quick emergency response system. Fire safety equipment and first aid available. Emergency contact numbers displayed.

Secure Lockers

Individual lockers provided for storing valuables. Additional security for your important belongings.

Convenience

Laundry

Professional laundry service available. Washing machines and dryers accessible. Ironing facilities provided.

Parking

Secure parking space available for two-wheelers and four-wheelers. CCTV monitored parking area.

Study Area

Dedicated quiet study room with proper lighting and seating. Perfect environment for focused studying.

Common TV Area

Common room with TV for relaxation and entertainment. Social space for residents to interact.

Reception

24/7 reception desk for assistance. Mail and package handling. Visitor management.

Printing Facility

Printing and photocopying services available. Convenient for students and professionals.
